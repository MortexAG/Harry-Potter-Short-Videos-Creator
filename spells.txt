
1-Aberto : Opens locked doors

2-Accio : Summons objects

3-Aguamenti : Summons water

4-Alohomora : Unlocks objects

5-Anapneo : Clears someone's airway

6-Aparecium : Reveals secret written messages

7-Apparate : A non-verbal transportation spell that allows a witch or wizard to instantly travel on the spot and appear at another location (disapparate is the opposite)

8-Ascendio : Propells someone into the air

9-Avada Kedavra : Also known as The Killing Curse, the most evil spell in the Wizarding World; one of three Unforgivable Curses; Harry Potter is the only known witch or wizard to survive it

10-Avis : Conjures a small flock of birds

11-Bat : Bogey Hex - Turns the target's boogers into bats

12-Bombardo : Creates an explosion

13-Brackium Emendo : Heals broken bones

14-Capacious Extremis : Known as the Extension Charm, it's a complicated spell that can greatly expand or extend the capacity of an object or space without affecting it externally

15-Confundo : Known as the Confundus Charm, it causes confusion of the target

16-Conjunctivitis Curse : Affects the eyes and sight of a target

17-Crinus Muto : Changes hair color and style

18-Crucio : One of three Unforgivable Curses, it causes unbearable pain in the target

19-Diffindo : Used to precisely cut an object

20-Disillusionment Charm : Causes the target to take on the appearance of its surroundings

21-Disapparate : A non-verbal transportation spell that allows a witch or wizard to instantly travel on the spot and leave for another location (apparate is the opposite)

22-Engorgio : Causes rapid growth in the targeted object

23-Episkey : Heals minor injuries

24-Expecto patronum : The Patronus Charm is a powerful projection of hope and happiness that drives away Dementors; a corpeal Patronus takes the the respective animal form of the caster, while a non-corpeal appears as a wisp of light; at 13, Harry Potter was the youngest known witch or wizard to prouduce a corpeal Patronus

25-Erecto : Allows a witch or wizard to build a structure, like a tent

26-Evanesco : Vanishes objects

27-Expelliarmus : Forces an opponent to drop whatever's in their possession

28-Ferula : A healing charm that conjures wraps and bandages for wounds

29-Fidelius Charm : A complex charm that conceals a secret into the soul of a chosen "Secret Keeper". If a location is the subject of concealment, it becomes undetectable to others

30-Fiendfyre Curse : Conjures destructive, enormous enchanted flames

31-Finite Incantatem : A general counter-spell that's used to reverse or counter already cast charms

32-Furnunculus Curse : A jinx that causes a breakout of boils or pimples

33-Geminio : Duplicates objects

34-Glisseo : Transforms a staircase into a slide

35-Homenum Revelio : Reveals the presence of another person

36-Homonculus Charm : Detects anyone's true identity and location on a piece of parchment; used to create the Marauder's Map

37-Immobulus : Immobilises living targets

38-Impedimenta : A temporary jinx that slows the movement of the target

39-Incarcerous : Conjures ropes

40-Imperio : One of the three Unforgivable Curses, it places the target under the complete control of the caster

41-Impervius : Makes an object waterproof

42-Incendio : Conjures flames

43-Langlock : Causes the target's tongue to stick to the roof of their mouth

44-Legilimens : Invading or navigating another's mind

45-Levicorpus : Levitates the target by their ankle

46-Locomotor Mortis : The Leg-Locker curse bounds the target's legs

47-Lumos : Illuminates the caster's wand

48-Morsmordre : Conjures and projects Lord Voldemort's Dark Mark

49-Mucus Ad Nauseam : Inflicts an extreme runny nose or cold

50-Muffliato : Creates a buzzing sound in the target's ears to prevent eavesdropping

51-Nox : Reverses the lumos charm, extinguishing a wand's light

52-Obliviate : Erases the target's memory

53-Obscuro : Conjures a blindfold

54-Oculus Reparo : Repairs eyeglasses

55-Oppugno : Directs an object or person to attack a victim

56-Petrificus Totalus : Temporarily freezes or petrifies the body of the target

57-Periculum : Conjures flares/red sparks

58-Piertotum Locomotor : Incantation used to bring to life inanimate objects and artifacts

59-Protean Charm : Links objects together for better communication

60-Protego : Casts an invisible shield around the caster, protecting against spells and objects (except for The Killing Curse)

61-Reducto : Reduces the target to pieces

62-Reducio : Shrinks an enlarged object to its regular size

63-Renneverate : Awakens or revives the target

64-Reparifors : Heals magical ailments like poisoning or paralysis

65-Reparo : Fixes broken objects

66-Rictusempra : A charm that disarms an opponent by tickling them

67-Riddikulus : Used to defeat a Boggart, the charm allows the scary creature to assume a comedic form, disarming it

68-Scourgify : Cleans objects

69-Sectumsempra : Inflicts severe lacerations and haemorrhaging on the target

70-Serpensortia : Conjures a live snake

71-Silencio : Silences the target

72-Sonorus : Amplifies the witch or wizard's voice

73-Spongify : Softens the target

74-Stupefy : The Stunning spell freezes objects and renders living targets unconscious

75-Tarantallegra : Aimed at the legs, causes uncontrollable dancing movement

76-Unbreakable Vow : A magically binding contract that results in the death of whoever breaks it

77-Wingardium Leviosa : Causes an object to levitate; but remember what Hermione said: "It's Wing-gar-dium Levi-o-sa, make the 'gar' nice and long.'"
